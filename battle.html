⚔️ Set Up Battle
Terrain Clash (Mindbug-style): no movement — strategies duel head-to-head, terrain decides. Attacker plays first, defender responds. Higher terrain-modified Power wins each clash; loser drops 1 life (start 5). Winner's card adapts +1 Power. Attacker may summon a Weirdo after round 2; a defender who survives 4 rounds may muster a terrain-native strategy.
